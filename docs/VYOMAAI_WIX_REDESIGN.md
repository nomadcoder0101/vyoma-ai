# Vyoma AI Wix Redesign Plan

## Goal

Redesign `vyomaai.in` as a polished product website for Vyoma AI, starting with Samruddhi's job search as the pilot use case.

The site should feel like a serious career operations product, not a generic AI landing page. The message is simple:

> Vyoma AI helps skilled professionals turn a complex job search into a focused daily operating system.

## Product Positioning

**Vyoma AI** is an AI-powered career operations system for professionals navigating difficult job-search constraints such as relocation, visa sponsorship, remote-market targeting, scattered applications, and weak follow-up visibility.

The first pilot is built around a senior AML / Financial Crime Compliance professional who recently relocated to Kuala Lumpur and needs an employer-sponsored Employment Pass role.

## Important Safety Boundary

Do not ask users for LinkedIn passwords.

The safe product flow is:

- User logs into Vyoma AI using Wix Members Area.
- User connects LinkedIn later through official OAuth if/when implemented.
- Until OAuth/API access is ready, Vyoma AI provides LinkedIn search links and asks the user to paste job/recruiter/profile links.
- The system drafts messages and recommendations, but the user decides what to send.

## Visual Direction

Use a clean SaaS/product style:

- Background: white or very light grey
- Text: charcoal / near-black
- Accent: teal, blue, or indigo
- Avoid heavy gradients and flashy "AI" visuals
- Use simple icons, clean cards, and clear hierarchy
- Tone: calm, strategic, trustworthy

Suggested palette:

- Charcoal: `#172026`
- Deep blue: `#12355B`
- Teal: `#0E7C7B`
- Light blue-grey: `#F4F7FA`
- Border grey: `#DDE5EC`
- White: `#FFFFFF`

Suggested fonts in Wix:

- Headings: Inter, Helvetica, or Wix Madefor Display
- Body: Inter, Helvetica, or Wix Madefor Text

### Section 2: Problem

**Heading**

A strong profile is not always enough.

**Body**

Many qualified professionals get filtered out before they are properly considered. The issue is often not capability. It is poor targeting, unclear positioning, sponsorship friction, weak follow-up, or a job search spread across too many disconnected tools.

**Problem Cards**

1. **Sponsorship friction**
   Employers may hesitate when work authorization requires extra steps.

2. **Scattered applications**
   Job links, CV versions, recruiter names, and follow-ups get lost across spreadsheets and inboxes.

3. **Generic positioning**
   Strong candidates often undersell their most relevant proof points for each role.

4. **No daily operating rhythm**
   Without a clear daily plan, the search becomes reactive instead of strategic.

### Section 3: Solution

**Heading**

Vyoma AI turns the job search into an operating system.

**Body**

The system reads the candidate profile, resume, application tracker, target markets, constraints, and role preferences. Then it helps decide what to apply for, how to position the candidate, who to contact, and when to follow up.

**Feature Cards**

1. **Profile Intelligence**
   Builds a sharp candidate profile from resumes, experience, target roles, constraints, and market preferences.

2. **Job Fit Scoring**
   Evaluates roles by fit, seniority, sponsorship risk, location, market value, and likelihood of progress.

3. **Tailored Applications**
   Generates role-specific CV positioning, cover notes, recruiter messages, and follow-up drafts.

4. **Tracker and Follow-up Cadence**
   Keeps applications, contacts, outcomes, and next steps visible in one place.

5. **Daily Search Plan**
   Suggests which markets, companies, roles, and recruiter searches to focus on each day.

6. **Human-in-the-Loop Control**
   The candidate remains in control. Vyoma AI recommends and drafts. The human decides and sends.

### Section 5: How It Works

**Heading**

From job search chaos to daily execution.

**Steps**

1. **Build the profile**
   Import resumes, role preferences, market constraints, location, compensation, and work authorization details.

2. **Import the tracker**
   Bring existing applications into one clean dashboard.

3. **Prioritize opportunities**
   Score roles by fit, sponsorship risk, location, seniority, and practical next steps.

4. **Generate tailored material**
   Create resume variants, recruiter messages, cover notes, and follow-ups.

5. **Run the daily plan**
   Scan target markets, identify outreach targets, and keep follow-ups moving.

6. **Learn from outcomes**
   Adjust the search based on responses, interviews, rejections, and recruiter signals.
